It's Clear you belong at Stirling

Study in the heart of Scotland

Stirling combines the charm of a historic destination with the friendly buzz of a young population. It's a short train ride away from both Glasgow and Edinburgh, and is a gateway to the Scottish Highlands.

Your Year 4 fees covered

Students from England, Wales, Northern Ireland and Republic of Ireland may be eligible for £9,250 scholarships covering your Honours year tuition fees plus a £1,000 Clearing scholarship (terms and conditions apply). Find out about these and other scholarships.

Guaranteed accommodation

Apply by 31 August and you'll be guaranteed an offer of University-managed accommodation in your first year of study (terms and conditions apply).

Your education and social activities during Covid-19

We are busy preparing to make your first year at Stirling fun and safe whilst managing the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. Find out about our commitment to making our campus safer for 2021.
