Sign up for Clearing updates Clearing is open. Call now 01786 466166

Clearing for 2023 is now open. We're here to help you get ready for results day and find your university place.

Sign up for Clearing priority email updates and benefit from exclusive support, including: 

  • live Q&A sessions,
  • campus tours,
  • access to live chat with our clearing advisers, and
  • email updates with tips, clearing advice and access to our clearing course list.

Plus, you may be eligible for a guaranteed offer of accommodation and over £10,000 in scholarships. Use our course finder to Find Your Place at the University of Stirling.

View all Clearing courses for English, Welsh, NI and Republic of Ireland students

Find your happy place at the University of Stirling, Scotland

More than £10,000 in scholarships

Students from England, Wales, Northern Ireland and Republic of Ireland may be eligible for £9,250 scholarships covering your Honours year tuition fees plus a £1,000 Clearing scholarship (terms and conditions apply). Find out about these and other scholarships.

Guaranteed accommodation

Apply by 31 August and you'll be guaranteed an offer of University-managed accommodation in your first year of study (terms and conditions apply).

Support when you need it

We offer an extensive range of support services to help you fulfil your potential and make the most of your time at university. You can get advice about mental health and wellbeing, money issues, careers and much more.

Meet us in Belfast

Talk to our experts and discover Stirling at our pop-up Clearing and exam advice office. You will find us at the Malone Hotel Belfast from Thursday 17 to Saturday 19 August 2023.
