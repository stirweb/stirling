Undergraduate Welcome events

Get the most out of life at Stirling with our programme of Welcome events. Packed with information sessions and fun activities, our Welcome schedule will help you settle into campus life, introduce our wide range of support services, and help you make new friends. Get involved by exploring our range of events below. 

Welcome events that are specific to your course will be shown on your personalised timetable.

Big Welcome BBQ

Join us in Queen's Court on Friday 6 Sep from 13:00-16:00 to enjoy a free burger and drink. It's a perfect opportunity to meet and network with other new students.

Welcome Freebie Fayre

On Monday 9 Sep from 09:00-16:00 you can come and meet lots of student-friendly businesses that will be giving out freebies, discounts and more!

Library tours

Get to know one of the most important places on campus: the library! See all library tour dates and book your place.
