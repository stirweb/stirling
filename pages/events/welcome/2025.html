Welcome events - September 2025

Big Welcome BBQ

Join us in Queen's Court on 12 September from 13:00-16:00 to enjoy a free burger and drink. There's music and outdoor games as well. The BBQ is the perfect opportunity to meet and network with fellow new students.

Book your free BBQ place

Buy your Welcome Week Pass

The Stirling Students' Union Welcome Pass 2025 includes entry to a range of ticketed events held in Venue throughout Welcome Week. Includes the famous UV party, ABBA tribute night and party bingo! Passes on sale now.

Find out more

Pop-up Green & Blue Space

Passionate about sustainability? So are we! For Second-hand September we have a pop-up Green & Blue space running throughout Welcome Week in the Atrium to get affordable kitchenware, homeware and much more! Open daily from 11 to 25 September between 12:00 and 16:00.
