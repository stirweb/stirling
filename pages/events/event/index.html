Green Week 2025

Share a link
10 March 2025 – 13 March 2025University of StirlingFree

Green Week takes place every year and is an interactive week dedicated to sustainability, active travel, biodiversity and waste reduction. 

The main focus of this week is to encourage staff, students and our local community to adopt a greener lifestyle and take part in our eco-friendly events. From Natural Paper and Notebook Making workshops to BioBlitz sessions, there is something for everyone. So get ready to take action and join is at #StirGreenWeek!

More events will be added to the calendar as they are confirmed.

Tuesday 11 March: Sustainable and Active Travel Day

Wednesday 12 March: Zero Waste and Circular Economy Day

Thursday 13 March: Nature and Biodiversity

Sustainability is not just for Green Week. Find out more about how staff and students can get involved in sustainability.
