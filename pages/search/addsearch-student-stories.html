Student stories

Find out what life is really like at the University of Stirling from our students and graduates. Hear about their student experience and discover how they’ve grown both personally and professionally to prepare for a successful future.

We’re recognised for our stunning campus, quality teaching and friendly multi-cultural community. University opens up opportunities to reach your full potential. Don’t just take our word for it, our students have stories worth hearing.

From coaching at Arsenal FC to launching their own hospital, Stirling graduates are making their mark on the world in incredible ways.
