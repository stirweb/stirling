Postgraduate study

Complete one of our Masters courses in just one year to achieve your career objectives and realise your life ambitions. We have 90 postgraduate degrees for you to choose from.

Types of taught courses

At Stirling, we’re all about choice. We offer a range of qualifications including MSc, MA, MRes and MBA. Most of our Masters courses take one year to complete.

Research degrees

We offer Master of Philosophy (MPhil), Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) and Professional Doctorates. See which research degree is right for you and your career ambitions.

Postgraduate funding

Get more information about our wide range of postgraduate loans, scholarships and funding opportunities.

How to apply

Once you’ve found the perfect postgraduate course for you, the application process couldn’t be easier.

Online Masters courses

Our online postgraduate courses offer a great level of teaching and support, and are perfect to fit in around a busy lifestyle. Take your career to the next level, or in a new direction.

Postgraduate progression courses

For international students who don't yet meet the requirements for direct entry onto a postgraduate course, our progression and English language courses can help you reach your potential.
