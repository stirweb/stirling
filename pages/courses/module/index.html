Content and aims

Module overview

Accounting is a profession at the heart of modern business and the economy. While the focus of much accounting education is on the technical aspects of professional knowledge, this can sometimes downplay the subject's inherently social and societal role.

Learning outcomes

After successful completion of this module, you'll be able to:

  • demonstrate a detailed knowledge and understanding of contemporary issues in academic accounting and finance research;
  • deploy independent research skills to collate materials relevant to a specialist topic in accounting and finance;
  • communicate knowledge of a specialist topic in accounting and finance through an oral presentation to an informed audience.
