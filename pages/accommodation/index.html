Overview

John Forty's Court is located off campus next to the historic Stirling Bridge. It's on the main bus route to Stirling town centre and the University. John Forty's Court is a popular development offering 287 single bedrooms. Each flat within John Forty's Court sleeps seven students and offers a spacious open plan kitchen, dining and sitting area. Your room will include a 3ft single bed, study desk and chair, wardrobe and drawers.

What's included

The following amenities are included as standard with this accommodation:

  • Heating, lighting and electricity
  • WiFi
  • Housekeeping assistance
  • Room and contents insurance
