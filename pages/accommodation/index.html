Overview

These modern two and three bedroom family flats are located on the north side of the University campus. Each flat has an open plan kitchen/living room with television, cooker, fridge, freezer, microwave, toaster and kettle, and a bathroom with bath and overhead shower. Your flat will include a 5ft king-size bed, 3ft single beds, a study desk and chair, wardrobe and drawers.

What's included

The following amenities are included as standard with this accommodation:

  • Heating, lighting and electricity
  • Housekeeping assistance (except in Alexander Court Family Flats)
  • WiFi
  • Television licence (for communal TV)
  • Room and contents insurance

How to apply for accommodation

We make it easy to apply for accommodation through our online application portal. Regardless of whether you're an undergraduate, postgraduate or visiting student, the portal allows you to select and prioritise the places you'd most like to live. If you're a new or visiting student, you'll be sent login details for the portal after accepting your offer to study at Stirling.
