On-campus Open Days

Join us in-person and explore our amazing campus facilities and beautiful surroundings. Our on-campus Open Day will give you a chance to attend presentations, meet staff, visit accommodation and much more.

  • Explore campus: Join one of our guided tours or take a look around at your own pace.
  • Presentations: Find out more about fees and funding, meet our students and get an introduction to university life.
  • Meet our experts: Discover more about our courses and chat with Course Directors and lecturers.

Details of our next on-campus Postgraduate Open Day will be available soon.

Register for updates

Postgraduate Webinars

Watch our series of on-demand online information sessions for postgraduate students to learn more about what it’s like to be a student at the University of Stirling. The sessions take you through everything from advice on studying with us, the support we provide and preparing for your career after graduating.

Sessions include:

  • Accommodation
  • Careers and employability
  • Student visas and immigration
  • Student support services

As well as holding regular Postgraduate Open Days on campus, we also host a regular series of webinars. Some webinars cover general topics, such as the application process or life in Stirling, while others focus on specific courses or areas of study and give you the chance to talk direct to teaching staff.
