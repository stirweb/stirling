About us

At the University of Stirling, being the difference is in our DNA – providing education with a purpose and carrying out research that helps to shape society. 

We are an international university with pioneering spirit and a global reputation for high-quality teaching and research. We are passionate about creating impact in everything we do.

Stirling is a place where ability – not background – is valued.

Our vision

We aim to be recognised across the world as a University that addresses the needs of society through innovative and interdisciplinary research, learning and teaching of the highest quality, and by sharing our knowledge with the world.

See more of our vision and strategy

Our people

The core strength of the University of Stirling is our people. To build on our success, we invest in the best staff from around the world to deliver on our ambitious strategies for research, teaching and our international partnerships.

Discover more about our people

Work at Stirling

At Stirling, we are always on the lookout for people who have the talent and the skills to make a real difference to society. Could that be you?

Faculties and services

The University of Stirling is organised for learning, teaching and research into five faculties, each of which comprises multiple departments, institutes and centres. There are also a number of professional services directorates within the University.
