Study at Stirling

Start your undergraduate adventure at Stirling and apply by the UCAS deadline of 25 January 2023. 

  • Our degrees are flexible and focus on your future career.
  • Top 30 in the UK for overall student satisfaction in the National Student Survey 2022. *
  • Our stunning friendly campus is rated 1st in the UK and 5th in the World for its environment **.
  • We're ranked in the top 40 UK universities in the Times and Sunday Times Good University Guide 2023.
  • The Stirling experience is based on a welcoming and thriving undergraduate student community.

More than 170 single and combined courses to choose from

View all undergraduate courses

Somewhere to call home

Looking for the perfect home away from home? Our range of secure and affordable accommodation includes over 2,000 rooms right on our beautiful campus, as well as easy-to-reach off-campus residences. 

Live and study in a beautiful environment

The University’s 330-acre campus is home to modern and spacious study spaces, world-class sports facilities, an on-site arts centre and cinema, restaurants and bars, over 120 clubs and societies, and we even have our own castle!
