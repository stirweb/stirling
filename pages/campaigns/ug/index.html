Study at University of Stirling, Scotland

Start your undergraduate adventure at Stirling and apply by the UCAS deadline of 31 January 2024.

Stirling is a vibrant, historical city in central Scotland. We enjoy close access to dramatic landscapes and excellent public transport links to Glasgow and Edinburgh.

  • Our degrees are flexible and focus on your future career.
  • £20,000 in scholarships available for our students from the USA and Canada.
  • FFELP loans are available for USA students.
  • We're ranked Top 30 in the UK for overall student satisfaction (NSS 2022).
  • 1st in the UK, top 3 in the world for campus environment (International Student Barometer 2022).
  • We are Scotland's University for Sporting Excellence with world-ranking sports facilities.
  • We'll guarantee you an offer of university-managed accommodation in your first year.

More than 170 single and combined courses to choose from

Honours degree courses in Scotland are four years, giving you the flexibility to put you in control of your education. You can try out new subjects, combine the ones that interest you or even change your mind about what you’d like to graduate in.

Some of our courses also offer advanced entry routes that enable students to gain exemption from Year 1 of study and go directly into Year 2 or sometimes 3.

View all undergraduate courses

Why Stirling?

£20,000 of scholarships and FFELP loans available

Eligible students from USA receive £20,000 through our Undergraduate USA Scholarship.

Eligible students from Canada receive £20,000 through our Undergraduate Canada Scholarship.

You can also apply for our International Sports Scholarship Programme, which is one of the largest high-performance sports programmes in the UK.

The University of Stirling is eligible to certify loan applications for the Federal Family Education Loan Program (FFELP). US students who are enrolled on a University of Stirling degree are eligible for loans and may borrow up to their calculated Cost of Attendance (provided by the University and updated for each year of a student’s course).

Find out more about financial aid for USA students.

How to apply

Start full-time study in September 2024 - all students can apply through the Universities and Colleges Admissions Service (UCAS) or Common Application. If you only want to apply to the University of Stirling, then you can apply to us directly for a full-time course.

Start full-time study in January 2024 and January 2025 - you have options to start your degree in January on some of our courses. Applications for January entry must be made directly to the University and not through UCAS. See what courses are available and apply.

Further information on how to apply.

Accommodation to make you feel at home

We've got the accommodation to make you feel at home in Scotland and we guarantee an offer of University-managed accommodation in your first year of full-time study (see guarantee terms and conditions).

Our secure and affordable accommodation includes over 2,000 rooms right on our beautiful campus, as well as easy-to-reach off-campus residences. Explore our accommodation options to find the right option for you.

Graduate Visa - stay in the UK for up to two years after you graduate

International students who successfully complete a UK degree can apply for the Graduate Visa, which will allow you to stay in the UK for up to two years after you graduate, with no restriction on the type of work you can do.

Find out more about Graduate visas.
