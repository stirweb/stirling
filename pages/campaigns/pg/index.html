Postgraduate Merit Scholarship

Any UK student with a First Class Honours degree (or equivalent) can apply for a £2,000 scholarship to study full-time on any eligible taught Masters course.

Find out more

UK Alumni Scholarship

We offer a 15% discount for the first year of a full-time or part-time on-campus Masters degree for UK students who have completed their undergraduate degree at the University of Stirling.
